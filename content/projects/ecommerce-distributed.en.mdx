## Overview

A distributed e-commerce backend split into independent services — product catalog, cart, order, payment, and notification. Services communicate asynchronously over Kafka for domain events and synchronously over gRPC where a direct response is needed. Each service owns its own database.

## Stack

- **Node.js + TypeScript** — service implementations
- **Apache Kafka** — event bus for order-placed, payment-completed, stock-reserved events
- **PostgreSQL** — per-service datastore
- **Redis** — cart state and idempotency keys
- **gRPC** — low-latency inter-service calls
- **Docker Compose** — full stack orchestration for local runs

## Notes

The project is primarily a study in distributed-system tradeoffs: eventual consistency between services, handling partial failures with retries and dead-letter queues, and keeping services decoupled through schema-registered events rather than shared database tables.
