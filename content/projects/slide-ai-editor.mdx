## Problem

Creating professional presentations is a time sink. Users spend hours wrestling with structure, copywriting, and formatting — tasks that are repetitive yet require creative judgment. Existing AI tools either generate everything (no control) or assist with nothing (no speed).

## Solution

Built a **hybrid AI presentation editor** that blends generation speed with human creative control:

- **Prompt-to-outline** — describe your topic, get a structured slide deck outline generated by GPT-4
- **Slide-by-slide expansion** — each slide's content is generated individually with context awareness
- **Inline editing with Tiptap** — rich text editor with full formatting controls; accept, reject, or refine any AI suggestion
- **Real-time streaming** — Vercel AI SDK streams content token-by-token, making generation feel instant and interactive
- **Multi-format export** — download finished presentations as PDF or PPTX for use in existing workflows

The key design principle: AI proposes, humans dispose. Every generated element is editable, deletable, and refinable.

## Result

- **6x faster creation** — average presentation time dropped from 2 hours to 20 minutes
- **Streaming UX** eliminated the "waiting for AI" frustration — content appeared as it was generated
- **Seamless workflow integration** — PPTX export meant users could continue editing in PowerPoint/Google Slides
- **Positive user feedback** on the hybrid editing model — "feels like pair-programming for presentations"
