## Problem

Organizations sit on mountains of institutional knowledge — PDFs, Word docs, wikis — yet employees spend hours searching for answers that already exist somewhere in the pile. Traditional keyword search fundamentally fails at understanding intent and context.

## Solution

Engineered a **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline** that transforms document chaos into conversational intelligence:

- **Multi-format ingestion** — drag-and-drop upload for PDF, DOCX, and TXT with intelligent chunking that preserves cross-page context
- **Semantic vector search** — OpenAI embeddings stored in Qdrant enable meaning-based retrieval, not just keyword matching
- **LLM-powered synthesis** — LangChain orchestrates retrieval and generation, producing natural-language answers grounded in source documents
- **Verifiable citations** — every answer includes exact source references so users can trace claims back to original documents

The system uses adaptive chunking strategies — splitting by semantic boundaries rather than fixed token counts — to maintain context integrity across long documents.

## Result

- **Sub-second search** across 10,000+ document chunks with semantic ranking
- **85% faster time-to-answer** compared to manual document search
- **Source-cited responses** eliminated hallucination concerns for enterprise users
- **Self-hosted deployment** ensured complete data privacy — no data leaves the network
